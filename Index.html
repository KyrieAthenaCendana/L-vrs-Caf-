How to Reserve a Slot?

  1. Visit our official website or social media page (@l'vrsCafé).
  2. Go to the "Reservations" section.
  3. Select your preferred date and time.
  4. Fill in your contact details (Name, Age, Phone Number).
  5. Confirm your booking and wait for the confirmation in your email account.
  6. Arrive at least 20 minutes before your scheduled time to check in because it might take a few minutes to check your reservation at the counter.
